Backpacking: A Path to Freedom and Self-Reliance.

By Cory McKielNov 17, 2022

One foot in front of the other

With each step, bring your attention to the task at hand. The challenge isn't associated with any particular step. Some goals only belong to the consistent. Even when you cannot see the end, believe it is there, and put one more foot in front of the other.

A destination in mind

The greatest gift is choice. You can decide where to direct your mind, intention, and strength. Pick your desination wisely and remember, any choice is better than none.

Just like a snail

Move forward, bravely, into the world. And, just like a snail, don your livelyhood upon your back. With a patient slowness and a deliberate consistency you move toward your goals. The snail is small. Each rock - a mountain. Every branch - a log. But every snail is bigger than its body. Watch the sureness of its motion. The snail will reach its destination.

"Life is full of beauty. Notice it. Notice the bumble bee, the small child, and the smiling faces. Smell the rain, and feel the wind."
