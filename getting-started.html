So You Want to Give Backpacking a Try?

Start with the following links:

  1. Backpacking for Beginners - REI
  2. Backpacking Basics - Youtube
  3. Self Reliance Outfitters
  4. Go Ruck
  5. Backpacking - Reddit

Link One

The is an excellent article from one of the top suppliers for outdoor gear. It talks about everything including planning your trip, what to bring, and what to know.

Link Two

If you're more of a visual person you can enjoy this youtube video from an experienced backpacker.

Link Three

Self Reliance Outfiiters is another great place to grab some gear for the outdoors. They also offer outdoor survival classes to prepare you for any emergencies you may face out in the wild.

Link Four

With all the miles you'll be putting on your feet, a good pair of boots goes a long way. I can't recommend Go Ruck boots enough.

Link Five

Finally, get interactive with the backpacking community and check out the backpacking reddit. There are plenty of interesting posts and it is a great place to post a question or find friends in your area.
